JOIN US

At EcoGo, we deeply take our locations into consideration. We value locations and businesses that are striving towards an eco-friendly lifestyle. Please join us..

IF YOUR BUSINESS:

  1. Demonstrates a commitment to an envrionmentally sustainable future.
  2. Delivers more than just profits, but uses eco-friendly practices and strategies from recycling to sourcing local products.
  3. Strives to have a positive impact on the environment and the community.
  4. Considers the environmental impact of their workers, customers and suppliers.

IF YOUR RESTAURANT:

  1. Is engaged in protecting the environment.
  2. Serves fresh organic ingredients that is locally sourced and considers on-site food production.
  3. Produces minimal packaging waste and serves food in recycled content packaging.
  4. Serves many vergetarian and vegan options.
