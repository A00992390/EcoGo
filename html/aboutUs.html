TO FOSTER A CULTURE OF WALKING, CYCLING AND SOCIAL INTERACTION

More than a Trip Planner

At EcoGo, our goal is to become the most environmentally progressive trip planner on the planet. We don’t want to just reduce the negative impact of pollution and plastic waste, we want to use EcoGo as a vehicle for change. We are the city’s number one eco-friendly travel planner that provides eco-friendly travel destinations and activities for locals and tourists. Our purpose is to foster a healthy culture of walking, cycling, and social interaction.
